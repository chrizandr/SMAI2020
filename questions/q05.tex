  ${\bf b}$ is another such vector. Consider the matrix:
     \[ {\bf A} = \sum_{i=1}^k {\bf a}_i {\bf a}_i^T + \sum_{i=k+1}^d {\bf b}{\bf b}^T\]
   What is the effective rank of ${\bf A}$
   \begin{enumerate}[label=(\Alph*)]
      \item $k$
      \item $k+1$
      \item $d$
      \item $1$
      \item none of the above
     \end{enumerate}
\end{frame}
